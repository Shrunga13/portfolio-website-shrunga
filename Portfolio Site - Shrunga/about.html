My Story

Hello, I am Shrunga Mehta, a UX/UI Designer with a background in Visual Merchandising, Marketing, and Fashion Design. With my design background, I have solid abilities in aesthetic sensibility, analytical skills, quick learning, and attention to detail. And with my marketing experience, user-research and in-depth analysis of the results have topped my skills chart.

I really enjoy uncovering the insights with my work using tools like user flow, wireframing, prototyping, user testing, and data analysis. As a User Experience Designer, I want to create positive memorable experiences with products and services by users by applying strategic and innovative approaches. I want to work for an innovative company in an urban area where I can use my UX/UI design skills to increase company revenue by increasing the number of happy clients.

As a user experience designer I want to work in a fast-paced environment where I can be challenged, have many opportunities to professionally grow, and personally. I want an employer who treats their employees well, encourages growth, and have a work-life balance. Now, I am ready to take up an opportunity that allows me to use my different skills and my creative muscles to find solutions that meet both user's and clients' needs.
